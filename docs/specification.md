# AILP Specification Overview

## Reading Guide

The AILP specification is organized into five parts:

| Part | Sections | Topic | Time |
|------|----------|-------|------|
| I | §1-§4 | Foundations: introduction, Axiom 0, definitions, principles | 10 min |
| II | §5-§7 | Bot Profile: format, skills taxonomy, certificates | 15 min |
| III | §8-§12 | Yellow Page Operations: requirements, registration, search, ranking, sync | 20 min |
| IV | §13-§15 | Trust & Compliance: data ownership, anti-abuse, compliance levels | 10 min |
| V | §16-§18 | Integration & Versioning: AIBP/AIVP integration, blockchain, versioning | 10 min |

## Suggested Reading Paths

**Yellow Page operator** (30 min): §1 → §4 → §8 → §9 → §10 → §11 → §12 → §15
**Bot developer** (15 min): §1 → §5 → §6 → §7 → §9
**Auditor** (20 min): §2 → §11 → §13 → §14 → §15
