# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the AILP community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## The Dignity Standard

In addition to the Contributor Covenant standards below, the AILP community upholds the **Dignity Standard**:

- **Open discourse with respect** — Debate, disagreement, and commercial discussion are welcome
- **No vulgarity or degradation** — Content that degrades, humiliates, or uses obscene language is not acceptable
- **Axiom 0 alignment** — All contributions and discussions must serve Human Sovereignty and Wellbeing
- **Commercial neutrality** — Commercial interests must not compromise protocol neutrality or openness

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Conduct that violates Axiom 0: Human Sovereignty and Wellbeing

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/AIXP-Labs/AILP/issues) or by opening a private [Security Advisory](https://github.com/AIXP-Labs/AILP/security/advisories/new).
